Online Shoe Reselling Site

NYEK SNEAKER SHOP

An automated marketplace and authentication engine connecting independent sellers with global collectors, specializing in deadstock and limited-edition releases.

Problem

The "manual" era of shoe reselling relied on risky peer-to-peer transactions. Sellers struggled with inconsistent pricing due to rapid market shifts, while buyers faced a high volume of counterfeit products, leading to low trust and lost revenue.

Solution

We launched a centralized marketplace that integrates real-time global market data to provide sellers with accurate, dynamic pricing suggestions. To eliminate fraud, the platform utilizes a hybrid authentication model combining AI-driven image recognition with expert physical inspections. This secure ecosystem uses an escrow payment system that only releases funds once a pair is verified, effectively cutting chargebacks by 88%.
